Services

Check out our list of quality & affordable services

Annual maintenance contract

Having your property covered under our Annual Maintenance Contracts gives you round the clock 24/7 365 days access to our team with an emergency* response time of 60 Minutes for any maintenance issue you may face. AMC assures you complete peace of mind. We have tailored our AMC into 3 different packages to fit your various requirements being the owner or the tenant of the property.

AC maintenance

If you wrote a list of the top 5 things that are absolutely crucial to maintaining your quality of life in a country with a climate like that of the UAE, wouldn’t air conditioning be at least number five on the list? Could you live without it? Well, like a car, air conditioners need regular maintenance to maintain proper performance. With expert maintenance, they will work more economically and be more dependable for years to come. Our specialist teams and wide-ranging experience at KNOX means that we cover every aspect of AC installation, repair, and maintenance.

AC duct cleaning

Is there a musty smell in your property and you’re wondering where it’s coming from? The chances are that it’s probably the result of dirty and/or partially blocked AC ducts. The pictures below speak for themselves. Whereas many companies only vacuum the air ducts (which does not guarantee a thorough cleaning) the professionals at KNOX will clean and disinfect your AC ducts with our state of the art machinery, giving you a cleaner and purer environment.

Electrical services

Electrical fittings are crucial for home safety and utility. They can save power, prevent fire hazards, and prolong the lifespan of electronic equipment if installed correctly. While DIY electrical work may seem appealing, it can be dangerous and lead to costly mistakes. It's best to hire a professional electrician for this specialized task. KNOX employs highly experienced electricians to ensure safe and proper installation.

Plumbing

Plumbing is a highly specialized discipline requiring the right skills and experience, so our advice to any home owner is … leave it to the experts. KNOX only use highly experienced plumbers in our professional plumbing team. We can meet all your small plumbing repair needs, from the replacement of tap washers, to repairing leaking gate valves.

Carpentry

Installation of replacement doors and windows are not projects for the amateur handyman. It is important that new windows and doors be fitted and installed correctly to avoid any damage to your home, and to provide the right fit, which works properly time and time again. KNOX only use highly experienced craftsmen for this highly specialised skill. We will carry out repairs and replacements to all kinds of UPVC, Carpentry, aluminium and glass doors/windows, from a small hinge to a whole new partition.!

Masonry

At Knox we undertake all sort of masonry job ranging from minor fixing of tiles to changing the whole tiles of the villa, cladding jobs, external granite fixing jobs etc.

Painting

At KNOX we undertake all sort of painting jobs ranging from minor touch up, to full room internal / external painting, painting of wooden doors, pergolas, Gazebos etc.

Cleaning

KNOX provides specialised water tank cleaning capable of cleaning tanks from small single piece moulded plastic tanks such as those found in lofts to large concrete supply reservoirs. All staff is highly trained to carry out the work.

Renovations/refurbishments

As time rolls on, our homes naturally suffer from the daily wear and tear of normal family life – the décor becomes a bit tired and drab – the kitchen that looked so modern when you first moved in now looks old fashioned – and the bathroom is on its last legs. Solution – a KNOX makeover. At Knox we have a team of dedicated home improvement specialist designers who can work with you to transform your home from top to bottom, or just focus on making good that one room that’s crying out for attention.

Landscape works

Do you dream of the thought of sitting, relaxing, and enjoying the peace and tranquillity of your garden, but struggle to even find the time to keep it in shape? Our professional landscape services are fully comprehensive, whether it’s just keeping your garden looking trim, neat and tidy, or designing the garden of your dreams. We’ll do all that for you, so that all you have to do is sit, relax, and enjoy!

Swimming pool works

Maintaining a swimming pool is a quite complex activity and really requires the services of a professional. KNOX specialise in swimming pool maintenance, taking away all the hassle of ensuring that the pool and its environment are clean and healthy, so that all you have to do is enjoy it!

Interior design / Garden design

Whether you seek to transform a small space to include lovely plants and flowers or want to build a grand outdoor garden to entertain in, the professional designers at aml are ready to help bring your vision to life.
